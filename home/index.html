Memorable Music for Games.



I specialize in creating Memorable, melody-driven music that helps your game stand out. Inspired by classic Japanese game soundtracks, I focus on strong themes, clear musical storytelling, and music that gives every area, character, and moment its own identity.

What I offer:



Memorable Music for Games

Bouncy, fun, and memorable melodies designed to keep players engaged. For Platformers and Arcade Puzzle games, I try to focus on rhythmic clarity and simple but memorable themes inspired by Nintendo games. For JRPGs, I focus on versatility while keeping the same style.
Each track is mixed cleanly and ready to throw into your project.




Interesting Sound Design

Equally as fun and memorable sound design, utilizing a mix of Synthesis and traditional folley. This makes sound effects match the same feel as the rest of your game. I try to focus on matching the rest of the audio, so you can guarantee that the sound design will fit.
